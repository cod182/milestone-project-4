### Process

#### User

- Load Site
- Click a link to search for a vehicle or search for make / model of car
- View search results
- Reset search page
- Filter vehicles
- View results
- Click on a vehicle
- Click reserve vehicle
- Enter use details and card details
- Make payment
- View success screen
- Register for an account
- Click on Accessories
- Choose and accessory, add to basket
- Choose another accessory, add multiple to basket
- Click on bag
- Click Secure Checkout
- Enter details and make payment
- View success page
- View profile
- View order

- Click Manage
- Click Add Vehicle
- Fill out info, upload pictures
- Click Add Vehicle
- Click Update
- Change some text and add an image and change main image
- Click Update
- Click Delete

- Click Add Accessory
- Fill out info and add picture
- Click Add Accessory
- Click Update
- Change text
- Click Update
- Click Delete
